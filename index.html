Famous Calligraphy Artists:

Mohamed Zakariya was born in 1942 in Ventura, California, the son of art director Emrich Nicholson and Amy Aplin. Later he moved to Los Angeles with his family. Zakariya saw Islamic calligraphy for the first time in the window of an Armenian carpet store in Los Angeles. Zakariya traveled to Morocco in his late teens where he became fascinated with Islam and Islamic Calligraphy. After returning from his trip to the United States he converted to Islam.

Frequently Asked Questions:


Calligraphy is the art of beautiful handwriting. It involves the use of specialized tools, such as pens and brushes, to create decorative and elegant lettering.


There are several types of calligraphy, including copperplate, spencerian, brush script, and black letter. Each type has its own unique characteristics and is suited to different purposes and styles.


To get started with calligraphy, you will need a few basic materials, including a pen or brush, ink, paper, and a guide or light box. You may also want to invest in a few additional tools, such as a nib cleaner and a inkwell.
